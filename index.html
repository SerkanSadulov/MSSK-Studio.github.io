○The Meaning○

○A story about a man who lost everything○

○The story of Ethan Daniels○



"The Meaning" is a psychological horror game, where you should expect the unexpected. It's a story about a man who has lost everything and everyone.
In the game, you start with the main protagonist, Ethen Daniels, and immediately dive into his story. You'll view his life from his perspective and be submerged in the deep and sad reality of his life. You'll be drawn into Ethen's life and question the meaning of yours.
The game is designed to keep you on your toes and don't let go, so be ready for scary moments and hard to solve mysteries.
You can expect a thriller story with scary moments, moments full of joy, sadness, uncertainty and unexpected events.

○ Immersive Experience -Realistic graphics and audio, as well as minimal user interfaces, will provide a completely immersive experience that will keep you on your toes.

○ Unique storyline -a psychological horror story with an unexpected twist, so don't read below the title of the game.

○ Hidden Items -Secrets & Easter Eggs.

○ Hidden items hint towards a different reality.
